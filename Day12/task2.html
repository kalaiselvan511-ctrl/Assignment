Death note

Light Yagami is an intelligent high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook known as the Death Note. This notebook grants its user the power to kill anyone simply by writing their name while picturing their face. With a strong sense of justice, Light initially seeks to rid the world of criminals, but his actions soon turn darker as he becomes obsessed with creating a new world where he reigns as god.
