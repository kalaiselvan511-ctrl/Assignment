About Light Yagami

Light Yagami, a high school prodigy, discovers the mysterious Death Note—a notebook that grants its user the ability to kill anyone simply by writing their name. Driven by his vision of a world free from crime, he takes on the alias "Kira" and begins a dark crusade against evil.

As Light's ambition grows, his moral boundaries fade, leading to a psychological battle with the genius detective, L. This conflict between justice and power defines one of anime’s most iconic stories.
